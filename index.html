Welcome to Renacs Engineering Nigeria Limited (RENL)

Renacs Engineering Nigeria Limited (RENL) is a multi-engineering company incorporated in 1977, and registered with the Federal Ministry of Trade now known as Corporate Affaires Commission (RC. 22253). The company registered office is in Enugu, with operational branches and offices in Abuja and Lagos. RENL has developed an enviable reputation for customer service and satisfaction. RENACS ENGINEERING NIGERIA LIMITED is a building and civil engineering firm. The company is a full-service design, procurement, construction and project management organization. The firm has the ability to provide clients with a complete range of services including planning, technical studies, engineering design, purchasing as well as construction and project management. The achievement of this company can be attributed to its qualified and experienced personnel. In addition to its staff and associate engineers, the chairman of the company is a COREN registered electrical engineer (R. 119) and Fellow of the Nigerian Society of Engineers, the managing and executive directors are COREN registered civil engineers (R.6698, R.6697), and are members of the Nigerian Society of Engineers. Our Overseas contacts with A.B. Engineering of USA, allows us the advantage of the support and resources of a major American organization. RENL is registered with the Federal Ministry of Works and Housing as a category "D" firm in civil and building engineering. The company is also registered with the Nigerian Society of Engineers.
