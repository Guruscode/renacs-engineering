Renacs Engineering Nigeria Limited (RENL)

Renacs Engineering Nigeria Limited (RENL) is a multi-engineering company incorporated in 1977, and registered with the Federal Ministry of Trade now known as Corporate Affaires Commission (RC. 22253). The company registered office is in Enugu, with operational branches and offices in Abuja and Lagos. RENL has developed an enviable reputation for customer service and satisfaction.
RENACS ENGINEERING NIGERIA LIMITED is a building and civil engineering firm. The company is a full-service design, procurement, construction and project management organization. The firm has the ability to provide clients with a complete range of services including planning, technical studies, engineering design, purchasing as well as construction and project management.
The achievement of this company can be attributed to its qualified and experienced personnel. In addition to its staff and associate engineers, the chairman of the company is a COREN registered electrical engineer (R. 119) and Fellow of the Nigerian Society of Engineers, the managing and executive directors are COREN registered civil engineers (R.6698, R.6697), and are members of the Nigerian Society of Engineers.
Our Overseas contacts with A.B. Engineering of USA, allows us the advantage of the support and resources of a major American organization.RENL is registered with the Federal Ministry of Works and Housing as a category "D" firm in civil and building engineering. The company is also registered with the Nigerian Society of Engineers.

The key to all electrification projects is the power source. RENL can provide base load or standby power stations in diesel, mini hydro, wind power and solar power.
The provision of electricity to remote communities or smaller urban population is the key to development and industrialization, but more importantly it adds to the quality of life and leads to improvements in education and health. As the cost of the supply of electricity by transmission to remote region is generally prohibitive, unless a suitable line passes in very close proximity to the area, the only practical proposition for the provision of power is by generation in the area itself. In some cases, bulk supplies of power may be brought to or pass through the electrification project region at high transmission voltages and consequently, the voltage must be reduced to a usable level for the project. In these circumstances, a voltage reducing substation will be required and RENL can provide the entire installation, from take off at the bulk supply point, to output to the transmission line or distribution network.
RENL. is able to undertake indoor or outdoor substations and switchyards for the full range of incoming voltage up to a maximum of 330KV and reduce this to any voltage level to meet the project requirements. RENACS ENGINEERING NIGERIA LIMITED has successfully completed the Urban Electrification of some of the most difficult areas, and by so doing, confirmed its ability in this field. Urban electrification can take several forms but in principle, it is the provision of power and street lighting to houses, commercial premises and factories in densely populated area.
RENACS ENGINEERING NIGERIA LIMITED is also specialized in the implementation of electrification contracts for rural communities, whether they are extensions to existing networks or complete backbone or development networks, based upon island site generation or derived from established distribution. Whether the project is a conceptual requirement of a local authority or government or prepared scheme, R.E.N. has the ability to undertake the complete turnkey contract.

One of the problems facing most developing countries is the provision of good roads at affordable costs;
In Nigeria and particularly since the turn of the century, the priority on road construction and or rehabilitation has become more pressing than ever before. Any method of providing good roads at affordable costs is paramount to the development of any nation.
RENL introduced the first alternative method for building and rehabilitating roads with the use of Ionic soil stabilizer ISS 2500. ISS 2500 as the name implies is used in stabilizing soils in problematic areas to allow for a variety of uses on the land in question. ISS 2500 chemically alters the constitution of the soil thereby making room for better bonding between soil particles. The result is you have better compaction and the soil is impervious to water. ISS 2500 offers an economical alternative construction method for roads, high-ways, dam stabilization, farm road, parking areas, air field, erosion control and water proofing.
